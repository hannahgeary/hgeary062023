# Past Tense
| Word | -ar | -ir | -er |
| ---- | --- | --- | --- |
| yo   | e   | i   | i   |
| tu   |aste|iste|iste
| el/ella |o|io|io|
| nosotros|amos|imos|imos|
| ellos| aron|ieron|ieron|
---

## Verbs ending in -car and -gar
Buscar --> c --> qu --> yo busque

Pagar --> g --> gu --> yo pague
